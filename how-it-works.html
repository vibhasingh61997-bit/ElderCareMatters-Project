Step by Step

The Full Process

1

Intake

Describe the care need

Use our guided intake — a short chat or form. Tell us who needs care, what type of service, where they are located (ZIP code), how urgent it is, and your budget range.

Who needs care? What type of service? ZIP code Urgency & budget
2

Scoring

We score & match your request

Your submission becomes a lead. Our system automatically scores it for data quality and conversion intent, then finds verified providers in your exact area who cover your ZIP code and service type.

Lead Quality Score (LQS) Lead Intent Score (LIS) Geo-matched by ZIP → city
3

Matching

Providers are notified

Verified providers in your area see your request in their Lead Marketplace. They review your care type, location, and scores — then choose to purchase the lead to unlock your contact details. Max 3 providers per request.

Max 3 providers per lead ECM Verified only Geo-matched coverage
4

Connection

Providers reach out to you

Matched providers contact you through the ECM platform. You receive messages in your consumer dashboard and by email. You choose who to respond to — there is no obligation to work with any provider.

Platform-mediated messaging Email notifications No obligation
5

Decision

You choose the right fit

Review provider profiles, credentials, and ratings. Reply to messages, ask questions, and make the decision that's right for your family — entirely on your own timeline.

View provider profiles Read verified reviews Decide on your terms

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get matched? ▼
Most families receive their first provider response within 24–48 hours. Urgent requests are typically responded to faster.
Is this really free for families? ▼
Yes — completely free. Providers pay a subscription and per-lead fee. There is no cost or credit card required from families at any stage.
How many providers will contact me? ▼
A maximum of 3 providers can purchase any single lead. You get meaningful outreach without being overwhelmed.
What does "ECM Verified" mean? ▼
ECM Verified means our team has reviewed the provider's business licence and credentials before they are listed on the platform. It is a baseline trust signal.
What types of providers are on the platform? ▼
Home care agencies, assisted living facilities, memory care facilities, elder law attorneys, geriatric care managers, and hospice providers.
