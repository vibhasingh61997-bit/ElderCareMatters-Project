How ElderCareMatters Works

A guided, transparent process — always free for families.

1

You describe the care need

Use our guided intake — a short chat or form. You tell us who needs care, what type of service, where they are located (ZIP code), how urgent it is, and your budget range. Takes under 2 minutes.

2

We score and match your request

Your submission becomes a lead. Our system automatically scores it for data quality and conversion intent, then finds providers in your area who cover your ZIP code and service type. Geography is the primary matching layer.

3

Providers are notified

Verified providers in your area see your request — your care type, city, urgency, and budget range — before deciding to respond. Your full contact info stays hidden until a provider purchases your lead.

4

Providers reach out through the platform

Providers who match your needs send a message through ElderCareMatters. You receive it in your consumer dashboard and via email. Your personal contact info stays protected until you choose to share it directly.

5

You choose who to work with

You are never obligated to work with anyone. Review provider profiles, credentials, and messages. Then connect directly with the one that feels right for your family.
