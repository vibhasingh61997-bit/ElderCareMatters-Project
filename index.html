Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service free for families?
Yes, completely free. ElderCareMatters is funded by the providers who subscribe to our platform. You will never be charged or asked for payment.
How are providers verified?
Every provider undergoes a review of business credentials, licensing, and references before receiving the ECM Verified badge. We also monitor reviews to maintain quality standards.
How does the chatbot matching work?
Our care advisor chatbot collects your care type, location, urgency, budget, and contact details. This becomes a structured request matched to providers in your geo area who serve your specific care category.
Do elder care laws vary by state?
Yes — licensing requirements, guardianship rules, and Medicaid regulations differ significantly by state. Our matching system accounts for provider location-based licensing, so you are always connected with legally qualified professionals for your area.
How quickly will providers respond?
Most providers respond within 24–48 hours. Featured and Premium providers are incentivised to respond quickly — their exclusive window to purchase leads expires if they don't act fast.
Can I update or close my care request?
Yes. Log in to your consumer dashboard to edit your request details, track provider responses, message providers directly, or close the request when you've found the right fit.
