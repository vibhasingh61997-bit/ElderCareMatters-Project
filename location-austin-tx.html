Austin Elder Care FAQ

Does Texas Medicaid cover assisted living? ▼
Texas offers STAR+PLUS Medicaid waiver that can help pay for home and community-based services including some assisted living costs. Eligibility is based on income, assets, and care needs. An elder law attorney can help you apply.
Are Austin assisted living facilities licensed? ▼
Yes. Texas HHSC (Health and Human Services Commission) licenses and inspects assisted living facilities. You can verify a facility's license and inspection history on the HHSC website.
What ZIP codes in Austin do providers cover? ▼
Most Austin providers cover Travis County including ZIP codes 78701–78705, 78710–78718, 78721–78735, 78741–78758, 78759, and surrounding areas in Williamson and Hays counties.
How do I check a home care agency's Texas license? ▼
Home care agencies in Texas must be licensed by HHSC. Search the Provider Search tool on the Texas HHSC website to verify any agency's license status and compliance history.
